Yosemite Valley Visitor Center

States: CA

National Park: Yosemite

Description: Year-round facility offering Yosemite maps and brochures, nature exhibits, a theater and a bookstore.

Operating Hours: 9AM-5PM

Address: 9035 Village Dr, Yosemite Valley, CA 95389

Phone number: (209) 372-0200

Directions Info: From San Francisco, take I-580 east to I-205 east to Highway 120 east (Manteca) or Highway 140 east (Merced) into Yosemite National Park.

Directions Info: https://www.nps.gov/yose/planyourvisit/maps.htm

Website: https://www.nps.gov/yose/planyourvisit/yv.htm
