West Thumb Information Station

States: WY

National Park: Yellowstone

Description: Offers information about the adjacent geyser basin on the shore of Yellowstone Lake

Operating Hours: 9AM-5PM

Address: Grand Loop Rd, Yellowstone National Park, WY 82190

Phone number: (307) 344-2876

Latitude/Longitude: {lat:44.415785, lng:-110.573375}
